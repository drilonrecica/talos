# 006 — AGPL-3.0-only licensing

## Context

Network-served modifications must remain available to users.

## Decision

License Binnacle AGPL-3.0-only. See [SPEC §2](../docs/SPEC.md#2-open-source-and-product-position).

## Consequences

Contributions follow the repository governance rules.

## Alternatives

Permissive and source-available alternatives are rejected.
